The Dataset folder should include:
-test
-train
-IndexFile_test.hdf5
-IndexFile_tra.hdf5

The test and train include the parsed images from the replayattack dataset from https://www.idiap.ch/en/dataset/replayattack
The two IndexFiles are the indices split for training and test data


The content in the Dataset flolder could be download from 'https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1nsTQ-3-hvgD_Y6rXdRYJmG4Y9wVrrUM-?usp=sharing'
